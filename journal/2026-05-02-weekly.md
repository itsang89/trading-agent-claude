- **Model inconsistency:** 5 sessions used "opencode/hy3-preview-free" instead of claude-sonnet-4-6. No observed behavioral differences, but signal validity across models is unknown.

After the add, avg_entry rose to $366.98, moving the trailing activation threshold to $403.68 — above the current high_close. Trailing stop was effectively deactivated. This is mechanically correct per strategy.md, but the protective floor was lost. GOOGL is now a 13% position protected only by the hard stop at $337.62.
